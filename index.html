About

I'm Tim, a driven Software Engineer looking to continue developing software through the world of a Full Stack Programmer. I've been honing my skills in top tech web technologies including HTML, CSS, Bootstrap, JavaScript, jQuery, Node.js, and VS Code.

Throughout my prior work as a Software Engineer for a large Healthcare IT firm, I gained valuable experience in SQL, Agile Development, Software Development Lifecycle, Requirements Gathering, Team Leadership, Communication, Complex Problem-Solving, Testing/Debugging, Project Documentation, Customer Support, and Microsoft Tools.

I am a dedicated and hard working developer with a habit of excellence. I have a history of completing simultaneous development and update projects on time and within budget to exceed expectations.

I will be completing the very rigorous Full Stack Coding Bootcamp at the University of Pennsylvania. I also have a Bachelors of Business Administration degree with a Major in Computer Science from Temple University.
